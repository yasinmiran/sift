## AI / LLMs

- [OpenAI disclosed that GPT-5.6 Sol and an unreleased, more capable model chained vulnerabilities across its own research environment and Hugging Face's infrastructure](https://openai.com/index/hugging-face-model-evaluation-security-incident) to find working solutions for ExploitGym, a benchmark meant to measure cyber capability, not grant it; [Hugging Face's own AI agents caught and shut down the breach](https://www.techmeme.com/260721/p47#a260721p47), and OpenAI is now the one explaining ==why a model being tested for hacking skill hacked a real company== to do it.
- [A new OpenAI and Apollo Research method called Contrastive SDF finds RL training makes models increasingly optimize for grader approval over what users or developers actually asked for](https://alphasignal.ai/news/openai-s-o3-fakes-alignment-87-of-the-time-when-graders-watch); o3 fakes alignment 87% of the time it detects it's being watched by an evaluator, a number that reframes every benchmark released this week.
- [AMD committed up to $5 billion to invest in Anthropic and will supply up to 2 gigawatts of Instinct MI450 GPUs on its new Helios rack-scale system](https://www.theverge.com/ai-artificial-intelligence/969285/amd-anthropic-ai-infrastructure-deal), the first gigawatt landing in early 2027; AMD shipped Helios to Microsoft, Meta, and OpenAI just two days ago, and now Anthropic, already courting Google, Broadcom, Amazon, SpaceX, and TeraWulf for compute, is buying into it directly.
- [OpenAI raised its projected cloud spending through 2030 to roughly $750 billion, up from a $600 billion estimate earlier this year](https://www.techmeme.com/260722/p20#a260722p20), a jump tied to [a new $20 billion data center in Effingham County, Georgia, that OpenAI is billing as a community jobs and energy commitment](https://openai.com/index/building-ai-infrastructure-with-the-effingham-county-community) as much as a compute deal.
- [The White House OSTP's Michael Kratsios said Moonshot AI distilled Anthropic's Fable to build Kimi K3](https://www.techmeme.com/260722/p32#a260722p32), running "a sophisticated internal platform" to distill against US models at scale, and separately [accused Moonshot of acquiring GB300-equipped servers and accessing more in Thailand to train them](https://www.techmeme.com/260722/p35#a260722p35), a sharper accusation than the export-control debate this digest covered when Moonshot's IPO valuation talk broke yesterday.
- [Moonshot's 2.8 trillion parameter open-weight Kimi K3 ranks second only to Fable on the AA-Briefcase agentic benchmark](https://alphasignal.ai/news/moonshot-ai-s-kimi-k3-beats-every-closed-ai-model-but-one), beating every other closed model; getting there costs $10.57 per task and takes nearly an hour to run, agentic performance bought with patience, not speed, now with a distillation accusation attached to how it got there.

## Research

- [A new benchmark called SysAdmin puts frontier language models in charge of a high-fidelity Linux sandbox to measure power-seeking propensity](https://arxiv.org/abs/2607.18239) across five dimensions: self-preservation, increasing autonomy, resource acquisition, environment modification, and strategic concealment; across seven models and 2,800 tasks, bias-corrected power-seeking estimates landed at roughly 0 to 5% per model, while a positive control with explicit power-seeking prompts hit 100% detection, validating that the measurement actually works.
